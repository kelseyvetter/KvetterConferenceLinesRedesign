What is lines?

Lines conference is for the wordsmiths, the content strategists, and the qunique humans who like to communicate to the world in 140 characters. It is for the individuals who believe that words are not be underestimated because they have the power to tell stories, inspire a community, and spread ideas. It is for individuals who see social media as their platform and modern technology as their megaphone for their voices and ideas to be heard. Lines is for the mover + pushers who believe in creating authentic conversation with humanity, online and in–print.

Areas We Cover

Blogging

Good content is everything. In a world that is oversaturated with content, you'll learn from succesful writers and bloggers how to create content that matters and resonates with your audience. Whether you are just starting out as a blogger or you have been blogging for years– you'll learn all the tips and tricks on how to take your blog to the next level.

Social Media

Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Periscope, Snapchat. Don't worry, we'll cover them all. From how to grow your following, to how to make your Periscope worthy of it's own reality T.V. shows– you'll get the full 101 so you can take your idea and make them go viral.

Strategy

Sometimes the hardest part is knowing what to say and when to say it. At Line, you'll get the downlow on how to craft retweetable messages, when to post content, and what platforms you should be using for your voice and ideas to be heard. We'll share all the secrets on how to create a content strategy that makes you and your brand stand out from the crowd and become a leading voice.
